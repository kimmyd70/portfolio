About Me

I’m a Seattle-based software developer who loves to travel and learn. My core skill set is team leadership in technical fields, a deep understanding of mathematical concepts, critical thinking, and problem solving. After a 25-year career as an Air Force Officer, I’ve spent the last 2 years doing freelance graphic design work. I’m passionate about social issues, specifically veteran- and economic inequality-centric issues, and I'd like to contribute to solutions in those areas. My skills and experience would be well suited in a small company or team interested in developing software to support veterans or artists groups, advocate for social justice, highlight environmental practices and concerns, or enhance the digital presence of locally-owned small businesses.
